# Vertical Applications of SnapSpace

Supporting multiple industry verticals highlights SnapSpace's role as a foundational substrate for controlling exactly-once, irreversible actions under uncertain conditions. The same deterministic execution model underpins very different sectors, demonstrating the platform’s flexibility and broad relevance.

## Safety / Alerting

SnapSpace guarantees that alarms, alerts, and notifications are triggered exactly once in response to qualifying conditions—no missed alerts, and no duplicates.

## Payments / Billing

Secures financial transactions against loss or duplication, ensuring each payment or bill is finalized precisely once, despite upstream uncertainty.

## Trading / Finance

Facilitates reliable order execution, settlement, and auditability, so irreversible trades are performed with exact traceability and no unintended side effects.

## AI Agent Execution Governance

Separates AI decision-making from deterministic actuation, providing a reliable commitment layer for agents tasked with taking real-world actions.

## Infrastructure Automation

Delivers consistency and safety for automation operations, ensuring each task (such as provisioning, scaling, or deprovisioning) occurs exactly once across distributed systems.

## Robotics

Enforces deterministic boundaries for life-critical or safety-related actions in robotics, supporting secure, audit-friendly operation in environments with uncertain sensor input.

## Driverless Vehicles / Autonomous Mobility

Provides robust commit boundaries for irreversible real-world actions such as navigation decisions or vehicle control, under the unpredictable conditions of autonomous mobility.

---

Across all these sectors, SnapSpace’s shared invariant is the exactly-once, deterministic execution of irreversible actions.
